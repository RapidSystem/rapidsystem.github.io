Features of Rapid ERP

Discover a single, affordable solution for managing your entire business.

Accounting & Financial Management

Automate the handling of all key accounting processes such as journal entries, payment voucher entries, receive voucher entries, account transfer entries, accounts receivable and accounts payable.

Generate standard reports from real-time data to improve your business planning and audit review processes.

Sales and Customer Management

Oversee the entire sales process and customer lifecycle more efficiently – from initial contact to final sale, after-sales service, and support.

Store all critical customer data in one place.

Generate detailed reports on all aspects of the sales process.

Purchase Management

Streamline procurement processes such as requisition requests, purchase order and returns management. Improve audits by matching documents, viewing information trails.

Generate integrated reports with real-time data and display them in various report formats or dashboards to gain an overview of what matters most to your business.

Inventory Control

Benefit from real-time synchronization of goods receipts and inventory levels in the warehouse to perform purchase planning with ease

Generate integrated reports with real-time data and display them in various report formats or dashboards to gain an overview.

Project Management

Plan your projects, closely track your tasks, collaborate with your team efficiently, and finish successful projects.

Keep every detail of a project centralized in real time so up-to-date info can flow freely across people, teams, and tools.

Human Resource

Connect every human resource process from hire to retire. Plan, manage, and optimize global people processes with one common data source.

Generate integrated reports with real-time data and display them in various report formats or dashboards to gain an overview.

Payroll Management

Design your payroll to meet international pay rules with a solution you can easily configure to meet your unique business requirements.

Streamline your payroll process with automated features. Spend less time processing payroll and focus more on managing your core business.
